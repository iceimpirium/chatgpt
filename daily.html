Monday - Declutter and Organize

  • Spend 15 minutes decluttering and organizing the most frequently used areas of your house,
  • Store toys in labeled bins or containers, making it easy for your children to find
  • Sort through mail, paperwork, and other miscellaneous items, disposing of or filing them as needed.

Sunday - Maintenance

  • Involve the whole family in a 15-minute cleanup
  • Assign age-appropriate chores to your children, such as setting the table, emptying the dishwasher, or helping with laundry.
  • Review and update the family calendar, meal plan, and any other household management

Wednesday - Bedrooms

  • Make beds and change linens, if needed.
  • Dust surfaces and vacuum the floors.
  • Organize closets and drawers, putting away clothes and other items.

Tuesday - Kitchen and Dining Area

  • Wipe down countertops, appliances, and dining table.
  • Sweep and mop the floor.
  • Empty and clean the refrigerator, disposing of expired items.

Friday - Living Room

  • Dust all surfaces, including furniture, shelves, and electronics.
  • Vacuum the floors, including under furniture.
  • Arrange and fluff pillows and fold any blankets.

Thursday - Bathrooms

  • Wash and fold laundry.
  • Change and wash bed linens, towels, and other household linens.
  • Put away clean laundry and linens in their designated places

Daily Habits

  • Make beds each morning.
  • Put away dishes after meals.
  • Encourage children to clean up their toys and belongings before bedtime.
  • Spend 10 minutes each evening doing a quick tidy-up of the house.
